Audio controls allow seek beyond end of Vorbis Audio recording, displays incorrect timing information

⇠ recording.oga (affected)
⇠ recording.mp3 (unaffected)

Steps to reproduce:

  1. Click and drag slider thumb for recording.oga towards the end of the track
  2. Continue dragging, beyond the end of track

  4. Repeat steps for recording.mp3
  5. Observe audio control works as expected
